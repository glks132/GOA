Pepperoni pizza


Dough

  1. 2 cups all-purpose flou
  2. 1 tsp instant yeast
  3. 1 tsp sugar
  4. ¾ tsp salt
  5. ¾ cup warm water
  6. 1 tbsp olive oil

Toppings

  1. ½ cup pizza sauce (or crushed tomatoes + salt)
  2. 1½–2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  3. Pepperoni slices
  4. Optional: oregano, chili flakes, extra cheese
Pepperoni Pizza is the most popular pizza type

Margherita pizza


Dough

  1. 2 cups all-purpose flou
  2. 1 tsp instant yeast
  3. 1 tsp sugar
  4. ¾ tsp salt
  5. ¾ cup warm water
  6. 1 tbsp olive oil

Toppings

  1. ½ cup pizza sauce (or crushed tomatoes + pinch of salt)
  2. 1–1½ cups fresh mozzarella (torn into small pieces)
  3. Fresh basil leaves
  4. 1 tbsp olive oil for drizzling
Margherita pizza was named after Queen Margherita of Italy in 1889, and its toppings—tomato (red), mozzarella (white), and basil (green)—represent the colors of the Italian flag.
