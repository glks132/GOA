Eau de toilette has a perfume concentration of about 10%, while eau de parfum has about 15%. Perfumes that are actual Parfum have a concentration of over 20%.


1. The best time to apply perfume is after a shower or after applying non-scented lotion. The more hydrated the skin is, the longer the scent will stay on.
2. Apply perfume on the pulse points of the body, such as neck, wrists, and back of the knees. These spots emit more heat that activates and maximizes a perfume's scent.
