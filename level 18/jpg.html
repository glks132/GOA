Jean Paul Gaultier Le Male Elixir

N1 Versacce Eros N2 Armani Stronger With You Intensely N3 JPG Elixir

Le Male Elixir by Jean Paul Gaultier is a Oriental Fougere fragrance for men. Le Male Elixir was launched in 2023. The nose behind this fragrance is Quentin Bisch. Top notes are Lavender and Mint; middle notes are Vanilla and Benzoin; base notes are Honey, Tonka Bean and Tobacco.
